IOT PROJECTS

  •   Project:

    A Leading Australian Work Safety Company

  •   Started on:

    July 2024

  •   Services Used:

    IoT Design and Architectures, Software Development

Project Profile:

The client is a pioneering provider of safety and asset management solutions, a durable tagging system designed to ensure compliance and streamline inspection processes for industries across Australia. With a focus on simplicity and reliability, the client’s solutions cater to businesses in construction, mining, and manufacturing, providing a seamless way to monitor and manage critical safety and operational data. Committed to innovation and practicality, the client combines cutting-edge design with user-friendly functionality, helping organisations maintain compliance, improve safety standards, and enhance operational efficiency. Connectified developed a custom IoT-enabled solution that provides real-time tracking, monitoring, and management of electrical equipment status and safety tags. By leveraging IoT technology, the platform offers a comprehensive view of equipment performance and compliance, ensuring safety standards are consistently met. This IoT solution not only enhanced the client’s ability to manage safety and compliance but also improved data accuracy and operational efficiency. With real-time monitoring and automated workflows, businesses using the platform can maintain their focus on core operations while ensuring the highest safety standards are upheld.




  •   Project:

    Custom Wearables Development

  •   Services Used:

    IoT Design and Architectures, Software Development

Project Profile:

Connectified introduced Watch Armour and Watch Guardian to redefine safety for individuals and workplaces. Watch Armour is a reliable alarm trigger device designed for discreet emergency alerts, supported by a robust web management portal for real-time monitoring and incident response. For users seeking enhanced capabilities, the Watch Guardian platform upgrades this security foundation by partnering with Samsung Watch technology. This collaboration combines the trusted features of Watch Armour with Samsung Knox management, enabling advanced device security, enterprise-grade management, and exclusive smartwatch functionality. Together, these platforms deliver scalable solutions tailored to diverse safety needs, from personal use to enterprise-level security.




  •   Project:

    Development of the Wireless Integration Board

  •   Services Used:

    IoT Design and Architecture, Security System Integration, Hardware Development

Project Profile:

Connectified created the Wireless Integration Board (WIB) to enhance the functionality of its wearable safety platforms, Watch Armour and Watch Guardian, while also integrating seamlessly with broader security systems. The board connects to local WiFi networks, allowing it to transmit alarms from wearable devices directly to monitoring stations and security systems, even in cases where existing monitoring services lack the necessary infrastructure to receive duress events directly. By enabling two-way communication, the Wireless Integration Board turns the wearable platforms into fully interactive security devices.




  •   Project:

    Integration With Australian Healthcare Technology Provider

  •   Services Used:

    IoT Design and Architecture, Security System Integration, Hardware Development

Project Profile:

In collaboration with an Australian healthcare technology provider, Connectified played a critical role in integrating advanced personal safety devices into the DirectWireless Private Network, enabling seamless and secure alarm signal transmission to the Central Monitoring Station (CMS) via the DirectWireless Alarm Receiver. The integration included fixed base units and mobile personal help devices, providing users with reliable emergency response capabilities whether at home or on the move. By leveraging the DirectWireless infrastructure, these devices ensured real-time monitoring, rapid alarm transmission, and uninterrupted communication, enhancing user safety and service reliability. To achieve this, Connectified facilitated end-to-end device integration, ensuring that alarm signals were transmitted efficiently over a dedicated, private network. Our approach included rigorous testing, network optimisation, and compliance with security standards, ensuring the highest levels of data protection and system resilience. By implementing this secure, scalable, and future-proof solution, Connectified enabled the healthcare technology provider to deliver enhanced emergency response capabilities while ensuring long-term reliability and regulatory compliance within the Australian healthcare sector.
