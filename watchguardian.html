Empower Workplace Safety with
WATCH GUARDIAN

The Watch Guardian platform is a seamless safety solution designed for Samsung Galaxy Watch6, Watch7, and Watch7 Ultra, ensuring real-time emergency response in dynamic work environments. Powered by Samsung and secured by Knox, it integrates smart alerts, remote device management, and automated security actions to enhance workplace protection.

Designed for high-risk environments like banks, hospitals, and offices, the platform empowers teams with real-time alerts, monitoring, and rapid response coordination. Whether it's discreet alarms from the smartwatch or automated security triggers from the Wireless Integration Board, Watch Guardian ensures comprehensive protection across your workplace.
